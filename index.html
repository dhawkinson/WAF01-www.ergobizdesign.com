About Ergo Business Design

You might expect an Interior Designer to be hanging drapes and wallpaper, but I love creating office environments that help people enjoy their work.

The psychology of color and design, when employed well, can actually contribute to your bottom line by stimulating people to work harder and more creatively. Isn't that what everyone wants when they furnish an office?

We can create that great environment with less cost by recycling high-end office furniture, and we also have access to beautiful new furniture at deep discounts, once again watching your bottom line.
